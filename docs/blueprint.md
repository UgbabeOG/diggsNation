# **App Name**: Diggs Nation

## Core Features:

- Tiered Memberships: Offer VIP, VVIP, and Premium subscription tiers with exclusive content and benefits, payments managed via Stripe.
- Exclusive Content Access: Provide exclusive content (videos, articles, behind-the-scenes footage) accessible based on membership tier.
- Personalized News Feed: Curate a personalized news feed for fans, featuring the latest updates, scores, and content related to Steffon Diggs.
- Fan Community: Enable fan-to-fan interaction through community forums and comment sections.
- Interactive Q&A Sessions: Host live Q&A sessions with Steffon Diggs, exclusively for VIP and VVIP members.
- Merchandise Store: Integrate an official Steffon Diggs merchandise store with exclusive items for members.
- AI-Powered Fan Insights Tool: Use AI to analyze fan engagement data and suggest content or experiences that might increase membership sales. Consider what content to provide depending on user actions, using community analytics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Crimson (#DC143C) to represent the passion and energy of the sport and athlete.
- Background color: Dark gray (#222222) to provide a sleek, modern look with high contrast.
- Accent color: Silver (#C0C0C0) for highlights, buttons, and interactive elements, adding a touch of sophistication.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font to convey the mix of digital engagement and human warmth.  This will also be used for headlines.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent various features and actions, ensuring they are easily recognizable and consistent with the overall design.
- Implement a dynamic and responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes, ensuring a seamless user experience across devices. Feature the subscription prominently on the landing page.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement and provide visual feedback, without being distracting.
